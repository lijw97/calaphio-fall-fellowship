Service Project Highlights

"Life's most urgent question is: What are you doing for others?"

- Martin Luther King Jr.


American Red Cross Blood Drive
Brothers work with the American Red Cross to host a blood drive across the UC Berkeley campus where each pint of blood can save a life.


Youth Tennis Advantage (YTA) BearTrax / Mosswood
Youth Tennis Advantage (YTA) is a non-profit organization centered on youth development through academic tutoring and tennis for educational and physical growth. Volunteers tutor students on their homework and practice tennis with them afterwards.


Berkeley Food and Housing Project
The Berkeley Food and Housing Project provides food and shelter to people in need. Volunteers prepare meals and serve clients.


Cal Star Yoga
CalStar Yoga is a UC Berkeley DeCal class initiated by brother Ben Lintschinger (JSC). This program strives to assist individuals with disabilities through yoga and massage therapy. Students help recipients perform exercises in Thai massage, Shiatsu, and other forms of physical therapy.


Halloween Carnival at Emerson Elementary
The Halloween Carnival provides fun and educational games and activities to children that aim to teach about good nutrition and healthy living. This is a chapter initiated event created by brother Michelle Gubatina (JY).


Playland-Not-At-The-Beach
Playland-Not-At-The-Beach is a free museum and family fun center that is a revival of San Francisco's Playland-At-The-Beach and other amusement parks of yesteryear. Brothers help to rebuild a magnificent amusement theme park where children of all ages can find a safe place to have fun.


Pledge Class Service Project
During each academic semester, the pledge class finds and initiates their own service project.


Project Open Hand
Project Open Hand provides nutrition services to people living with HIV/AIDS and other critical illnesses and to seniors. Brothers help to prepare and package food for people in need.

View our calendar to see more of our service projects. If you or your organization is interested in working with us, please email our VP of Service Elaine Do at service-vp@calaphio.com .
