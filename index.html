Fall Fellowship 2016

Alpha Phi Omega - Gamma Gamma Chapter

Welcome to Fall Fellowship 2016 at Gamma Gamma Chapter!

Gamma Gamma is excited to host this year’s Section IV Fall Fellowship! We have not hosted an event of this magnitude in quite some time, and we are looking forward to preparing an event that will be highly enjoyable for all our guests! As usual, this will consist of morning icebreakers, local food, roll call performances from each chapter, and the opportunity to meet brothers from all across our section.

Fall Fellowship is set to be held Saturday, November 12th, at the UC Berkeley campus. Sign up as soon as you can to avoid regular increases to the registration fee. From all of your Gamma Gamma brothers, we hope you to see you there!
