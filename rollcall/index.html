Alpha Phi Omega

Fall Rush 2016

"Be the light."

Alpha Phi Omega is an international co-ed service fraternity with over 85 years of college volunteerism and the largest fraternity in the world. Our chapter at UC Berkeley consists of over 100 active members who contribute thousands of hours of service annually. In addition to service we have numerous events during the semester to promote friendship and brotherhood within all chapters around the world.

Rush Schedule

WEEK ONE: WELCOME

  • Info Night 1: 1/26, 7pm Tuesday, 160 Dwinelle

    • Want to know what we’re all about? Come hang out with our active members at Info Night and learn more about who are and what we do.

  • Info Night 2: 1/27, 7pm Wednesday, 126 Barrows

    • Missed the first session, but still interested? We have a second info night just for you!

  • Cal Star Yoga (Service): 1/29, 1:30-3:30pm Friday, Meet in front of RSF

    • See for yourself what doing service with active members is like. Volunteer with CalStar Yoga to help those with physical and mental disabilities experience various range of motion and physical therapy exercises.

    Please email rush@calaphio.com if you are interested in attending as spots for service are limited.

WEEK TWO: SPONSORSHIP

  • MANDATORY: Meet the Chapter: 1/31, 11am-2:30pm, Sunday, 213 Wheeler

    • We want to get to know you! You'll also meet your sponsor here - an active brother that'll be your guide for the rest of rush. Lunch from Gypsy's Trattoria Italiana will be provided.

  • Project Open Hand (Service): 2/1, Monday, leave Berkeley at 4PM, leave SF by 7:30pm

    • The best way to get an idea of what we do is to come out and experience it for yourself. Project Open Hand is a partnership of volunteers, donors, and staff working to provide for people living with HIV/AIDS, Seniors, and Home-bound individuals living with serious illness. Free dinner included!

    Please email rush@calaphio.com if you are interested in attending as spots for service are limited.

  • Palate Testing : 2/3 Wed, 7pm-8:30pm, 213 Wheeler

    • Tasting with a twist - can you guess what exactly it is you’re eating? Invigorate your senses by tasting fun foods without looking!

  • Games and Desserts Night: 2/5 Friday, 7-9pm, 60 Barrows

    • We’re taking games and dessert night to a whole new level! Come spend a cozy winter night inside with us playing board games and feeding your sweet tooth. Snacks and hot chocolate will be provided!

WEEK THREE: PLEDGING

  • MANDATORY: Rushee Interviews: 2/7 Sunday, 10:30am-12PM, 187 Dwinelle

    • This is just a chance for us to get to know you better! Please come dressed in business casual. Please contact membership-vp@calaphio.com if you cannot make this event, but are still interested in pledging.

  • MANDATORY: Ritual: 2/9 Tuesday TBA

    • This marks the beginning your journey. Please come dressed in business casual.

In order to pledge Alpha Phi Omega, you must fulfill the following 5 requirements:

  • Attend 1 Info Night
  • Attend 1 Additional Rush Event
  • Attend Interviews
  • Attend Ritual
  • Meet the Chapter

As a general note, the more active brothers you meet, the better you can get to know our fraternity, so we encourage you to attend as many events as you can!

We look forward to meeting you! Please contact one of our rush chairs at rush@calaphio.com for any questions or concerns.
